Trout risotto

Ingredients

  • Risotto rice
  • Shallots
  • Smoked trout
  • Leek
  • Butter
  • Strong grated cheese
  • Veggie broth
  • Dill
  • Pepper
  • White wine

How to

  1. Chop the onions and the leek and add them to a pan on medium heat with melted butter
  2. While the onions and the leek cook, boil some broth
  3. Once the onions are transparent, add you rice, your spices and a bit of wine, and let the alcohol cook off
  4. After the wine boils, start adding the hot broth little by little and let the rice cook while stirring
  5. When it is almost done, add a bit more butter and a LOT of your cheese of choice
  6. Serve it and then add the smoked trout, preferably on small dices
  7. Eat!
